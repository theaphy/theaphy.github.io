Bench Consulting is a team of placemakers focused on helping hyper-local projects come to life to support vibrant, thriving communities.

We engage communities in all many phases of projects with a variety of goals and scopes, feel free to start a conversation to see if our skills are a fit for your needs!

our services

Allow everyone to make the most of the missed opportunities in the vacant spaces and underutilized places to create a vibrant, thriving, connected community increasing local qualities of life.

placemaking plan

Is your main street, neighborhood, business district or development project looking to add more vibrancy but don’t know where to start? Our team can help locate your areas of opportunity and engage your community to get you started on the right path to make your space a place.

single intervention

Whether we’ve designated a space for improvement through our Placemaking Plan or you’ve already found the space you’d like to activate, our team of experts will work with your community to activate the space that serves the needs and ultimate goals of your organization, community and city.

long term engagement

There’s ample opportunity in your main street, downtown or development project. Let our team work with you and your community to identify those opportunities and develop short and long term placemaking plans and interventions to support a more vibrant sense of place.

who we are

We’re a team with a passion for bringing communities together in collective efforts to build vibrant cities and towns across the world. We have backgrounds in design, law, nonprofits, grassroots organizing and have all traveled the world, learning what transforms our shared “spaces” into “places.”
